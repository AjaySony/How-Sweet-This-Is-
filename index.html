Snickers - The Ultimate Chocolate Bar

Snickers is a delicious chocolate bar with a combination of nougat, caramel, and peanuts, coated in milk chocolate. It satisfies your hunger and satisfies your sweet tooth!

Fun Facts About Snickers

  • Snickers was introduced in 1930 by Mars, Inc.
  • The name "Snickers" comes from one of the Mars family's favorite horses.
  • It was originally sold under the name "Marathon" in the UK and Ireland.
  • Snickers is the best-selling chocolate bar in the world.
  • It has been featured in numerous commercials, including the famous "You're not you when you're hungry" campaign.
